Quinoa-crusted Salmon

Description

This Quinoa Crusted Salmon uses quinoa and breadcrumbs for added texture, flavor and nutrition and pairs beautifully with a side salad.

It's packed with flavor and ready in under 20 minutes, making it an ideal weeknight meal!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz. salmon fillets
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoon seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 3 tablespoon quinoa
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon parsley
  • chives for garnish

Steps

  1. Whisk egg in a bowl, and in a separate bowl combine the breadcrumbs, uncooked quinoa, salt, garlic, and parsley.
  2. Next heat 1-2 tablespoon oil in a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Dip each salmon fillet into the egg mixture, and then directly into the quinoa mixture, making sure both sides are coated.
  4. Place salmon directly into the skillet skin-side down and cover the skillet with a lid. Cook salmon for 5-6 minutes, then flip and cook for 1-2 more minutes.
  5. Top salmon with fresh chives and enjoy!
